TITLE ECO-Code

<!-- Taken from http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/ECO-Code -->

<p>
ECO-Code (Encyclopedia of Chess Openings) is the term for an internationally used
system for systematization of chess openings. The chess variations will be presented
and evaluated with symbols. It doesn't use words, so that the presentation is
internationally understandable.
</p>
