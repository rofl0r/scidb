Supported chess variants

Scidb is supporting various chess variants, currently this is:

Normal Chess
Crazyhouse 
Antichess  (Suicide , Giveaway )
Losers  (variant of Antichess)
Three-check Chess 

In all chess variants Chess 960  and also Shuffle Chess  positions are supported.

For chess variants Crazyhouse and Three-check Chess Scidb is using required extensions for FEN (⇒ Forsyth-Edwards-Notation).
