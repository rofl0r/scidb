<dir>
  <li><p>Game history</p></li>
  <li><p>Help dialog (with a few help pages)</p></li>
  <li><p>New file selection box with extraneous functions:</p></li>
    <ul type="circle">
      <li>Delete database</li>
      <li>Rename database</li>
      <li>Duplicate database</li>

  <li><p>Revised export dialog</p></li>
  <li><p>Undo/redo in comment editor</p></li>

    <ul type="circle">
      <li>Additional scoring system "Bilbao" (three points for a win)</li>
      <li>Additional tie-breaks</li>
    </ul>
  <li><p>The export to .si3/.si4 now offers a selection of the PGN-Tags (a restriction of the tags is accelerating the position tree)</p></li>
  <li><p>Selection dialog of database symbols is revised</p></li>
