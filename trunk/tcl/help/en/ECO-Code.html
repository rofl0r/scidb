ECO-Code

ECO-Code (Encyclopedia of Chess Openings) is the term for an internationally used system for systematization of chess openings. The chess variations will be presented and evaluated with symbols. It doesn't use words, so that the presentation is internationally understandable.

  • Volume A: Flank openings
  • Volume B: Semi-Open Games
  • Volume C: Open Games
  • Volume D: Closed Games and Semi-Closed Games
  • Volume E: Indian Defences
